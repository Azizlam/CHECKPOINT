OUR ACTIVITIES Sports activities bring many benefits to the body. Practicing daily physical exercise can extend life expectancy, not to mention the immediate effect of sport, which releases endorphins in the body and provides a feeling of well-being. In addition to health, the motivations that lead us to enroll in a sports club are multiple and can evolve as we become involved in training programs: fitness, weight gain, weight loss, endurance, rehabilitation...
